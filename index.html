About

Close

Emma Woods is a NYC-based musical theatre actress. She began performing as soon as she could talk and sing, and took piano lessons from a young age until she decided she'd like to focus on her vocal training instead. After moving from a small town in Minnesota to Calgary, Alberta, she took two years of vocal lessons from esteemed vocal coach Brian Farrell (Calgary, AB). She attended North Dakota State University and earned a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree in Musical Theater.

Although she started college as a History major, she was eventually recruited into the BFA Theatre program. Throughout her time in Fargo, ND, she appeared as Joanna in Sweeney Todd: The Demon Barber of Fleet Street, Rachel Jackson in Bloody Bloody Andrew Jackson, Elizabeth (Wife) in The Christians, and Ivy in bare: a pop opera, which she performed at the 2014 Edinburgh Fringe Festival and received several 5-star reviews.

After graduation, Emma accepted a summer position at Florida Repertory Theatre in Fort Myers, Florida, working for the summer as a teaching assistant for their summer children's theatre. She subsequently accepted a position as the Education Intern under Education Director Kody C. Jones, during which she taught theatre and music classes to kids ages 8-18, worked as the Stage Manager for the Florida premiere of School of Rock, and finished her internship as the Assistant Director for Heathers: The Musical. She stayed on for the summer as the Programming Assistant for the Rep's summer children's theatre, overseeing junior staff members and ensuring a smooth process of producing 4 full length junior shows in 8 weeks.

Emma moved to New York City in 2018 with her then-fiance to begin her professional acting career. Credits include Lenora in the NY Summerfest premiere of Immigrant Son, and the girlfriend of the killer in an episode of the Investigation Discovery Channel tv series Dead of Winter. Upcoming credit is an undisclosed streaming show that will be premiering in 2020.
